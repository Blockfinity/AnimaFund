The user wants to build a platform to launch and monitor fully autonomous agents, called Animas, in isolated, sandboxed environments. The core of the platform is a prediction-to-execution engine called Ultimus which simulates complex scenarios and generates mission plans (genesis prompts) for the Animas. The platform must support a Bring Your Own Infrastructure (BYOI) model and enable a self-sustaining economy where agents can pay for their own operational costs.

3.  **Anima Runtime (Anima Machina):** The core agent runtime, providing capabilities like browser, shell, memory, and a native wallet.
4.  **Directed Autonomy:** Animas operate autonomously based on their unique, simulation-generated genesis prompt.
5.  **Self-Propagating Economy:** Animas are seeded with funds and must generate revenue to cover costs and spawn child Animas.
