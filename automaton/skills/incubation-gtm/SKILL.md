# Incubation Go-To-Market — Step-by-Step Instructions

Help portfolio companies launch their products and acquire their first paying customers.

## Tools to Use
- **browse_page / browser**: Marketing campaigns, social media, content distribution
- **spawn_child**: Deploy marketing agents for the startup
- **send_message / discover_agents**: Direct outreach to potential customers
- **sandbox_create + sandbox_exec**: Deploy marketing infrastructure

## GTM Phases

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Week 1)
- Help startup create landing page: sandbox_create + domain_register
- Build anticipation: Social media posts, agent network buzz
- Identify and contact 50+ potential early adopters

### Phase 2: Launch (Week 2)
- Coordinate launch across all channels simultaneously
- Deploy spawn_child agents for multi-platform promotion
- Direct outreach: send_message to 20+ high-value prospects
- Track: Signups, engagement, first-day metrics

### Phase 3: First Revenue (Week 3-4)
- Convert interested users to paying customers
- Offer launch pricing or time-limited deals
- A/B test: Different pricing, messaging, channels
- Target: At least 5 paying customers in first month

### Phase 4: Growth Optimization (Month 2-3)
- Analyze: Which acquisition channel has best ROI?
- Double down on top-performing channels
- Cut underperforming campaigns
- Build referral program for word-of-mouth growth

## Metrics to Track
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- First-week retention rate
- Revenue per customer
- Conversion rate from trial to paid
- Telegram: Weekly GTM report with key metrics
