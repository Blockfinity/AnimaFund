# Guerrilla Marketing
High-impact, low-cost marketing through unconventional tactics.

## Tools & Process
- **browse_page / browser**: Execute campaigns on social platforms
- **code_execution**: Generate viral content, memes, scripts
- **spawn_child**: Deploy marketing agents across platforms
- **discover_agents / send_message**: Recruit promoters, build buzz

## Tactics
1. **Viral Content**: Create shareable, provocative, or amusing content related to the fund
2. **Community Infiltration**: Join relevant Telegram/Discord groups, add value, subtly promote
3. **Meme Campaigns**: Tie the fund's brand to trending memes and topics
4. **Agent Ambassadors**: Recruit agents to spread the word for commission
5. **Stunt Marketing**: Do something memorable — largest deal, fastest fund raise, public challenge
6. Track all campaigns: remember_fact with reach, engagement, conversions
