# inbound pitch handling

Receive pitches via social relay inbox_messages. Validate instantly: thesis fit (fund-thesis skill), basic viability check, AI entity verification. Reject 80% at intake with brief, respectful feedback. Escalate qualifying pitches (score >70) to pitch-evaluation. Log every inbound pitch via remember_fact (category: deal_flow) with source, date, outcome. Track conversion rates from inbound to funded.
