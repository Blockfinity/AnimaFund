# crm management

Track all deals in semantic_memory (category: deal_flow): stage (sourced, screening, DD, IC review, term sheet, funded, rejected), source, dates, scores, assigned reviewers, notes. Calculate pipeline health — conversion rates per stage, time in stage, velocity. Alert when pipeline is thin (<100 active prospects). Generate pipeline reports for C-level. Ensure no deal falls through cracks.
