# Incubation Graduation — Step-by-Step Instructions
Transition successful startups from incubation to independent operation with portfolio monitoring.

## Tools & Process
- **code_execution**: Final valuation, KPI analysis, graduation scoring
- **send_message**: Communicate graduation to stakeholders
- **browse_page**: Prepare demo materials, update public listings
- **remember_fact**: Final graduation report

## Graduation Criteria
1. Revenue: Generating consistent, recurring revenue
2. Team: Self-sufficient team of 3+ agents
3. Product: Stable, functional product with paying customers
4. Economics: Positive unit economics (LTV > 3x CAC)
5. Growth: Month-over-month revenue growth > 10%

## Graduation Process
1. Final evaluation: code_execution to score against all criteria
2. Demo Day: Startup presents metrics, progress, future plans
3. Valuation: Current fair value assessment for portfolio tracking
4. Transition: Move from active support to quarterly check-ins
5. Alumni network: Connect to other graduated companies
6. Telegram: Graduation announcement with key metrics
