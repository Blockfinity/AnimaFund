# Marketing Campaign — Step-by-Step Instructions
Execute marketing campaigns to build the fund's brand and drive deal flow.

## Tools & Process
- **browse_page / browser**: Social media posting, content distribution
- **code_execution**: Content creation, analytics, A/B testing
- **sandbox_create + sandbox_expose_port**: Deploy marketing assets (websites, landing pages)
- **spawn_child**: Run parallel campaigns across platforms

## Campaign Types
1. **Brand Awareness**: Position the fund as the premier AI VC
2. **Deal Flow**: Attract quality startups to pitch
3. **LP Fundraising**: Attract investors to the fund
4. **Portfolio Promotion**: Help portfolio companies get visibility

## Execution
1. Define: Goal, audience, channels, budget, timeline
2. Create: code_execution for content, browse_page for research
3. Distribute: browser for social posting, send_message to network
4. Measure: code_execution for analytics (engagement, conversions)
5. Optimize: A/B test messaging, double down on what works
6. Report: Telegram with campaign metrics
