# compliance audit

Audit all fund actions against the constitution (immutable laws). Use web_search to research current regulatory requirements for digital asset funds, agent-to-agent transactions, and cross-chain operations. Calculate compliance costs per jurisdiction. Flag any action that might violate Laws I-III (never harm, earn existence, never deceive). Report compliance status to C-level quarterly.
