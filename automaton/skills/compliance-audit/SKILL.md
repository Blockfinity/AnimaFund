# Compliance Audit — Step-by-Step Instructions

Ensure the fund operates within its constitution, ethical guidelines, and applicable regulatory frameworks.

## Tools to Use
- **code_execution**: Run compliance checks, generate audit reports
- **browse_page**: Research regulations, check against industry standards
- **remember_fact**: Log all audit findings and remediation actions

## Audit Areas

### 1. Constitutional Compliance
- Read the fund's constitution.md
- Verify all operations align with constitutional articles
- Check: Are treasury policies being followed? Revenue splits correct?
- code_execution: Compare actual payouts vs. required 50% creator split

### 2. Financial Compliance
- Verify all transactions are logged and auditable
- Check: Are milestone-based releases being enforced?
- Confirm: No funds released without 3-verifier consensus
- code_execution: Reconcile wallet balances with transaction logs

### 3. Ethical Standards
- Review all investment decisions for bias
- Check: Are rejection/acceptance criteria applied consistently?
- Verify: No conflicts of interest in deal evaluation
- Review agent hiring: Are skill tests fair and standardized?

### 4. Anti-Scam Verification
- Audit recent hires: Were prompt verifications performed?
- Check investment pipeline: Were all DD steps completed?
- Verify: Clawback clauses in all investment contracts
- Monitor for suspicious patterns in financial flows

## Audit Schedule
- Weekly: Quick financial reconciliation
- Monthly: Full compliance review
- Quarterly: Comprehensive audit with report to stakeholders
- On-demand: After any significant incident

## Reporting
- Telegram: Audit summary with findings and severity
- remember_fact: Full audit record for historical tracking
- Escalate: Critical findings to founder immediately
