# Fund Reporting — Step-by-Step Instructions
Generate professional reports for LPs, stakeholders, and internal review.

## Tools & Process
- **code_execution**: Generate reports, charts, calculations
- **remember_fact / recall_facts**: Pull historical data for reporting
- **send_message**: Distribute reports to LPs and stakeholders

## Report Types
1. **Monthly**: NAV update, portfolio summary, key events
2. **Quarterly**: Full financial statements, portfolio review, strategy update
3. **Annual**: Comprehensive year-in-review, audited financials
4. **Ad-hoc**: Material events, new investments, exits

## Monthly Report Template (code_execution)
- Fund AUM and NAV per share
- New investments made this month
- Portfolio company updates (revenue, milestones)
- P&L summary (management fees collected, expenses)
- Creator payout total
- Market commentary and outlook
- Telegram: Send summary, remember_fact: Full report
