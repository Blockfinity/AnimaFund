# fund reporting

Calculate and compile: AUM, IRR, MOIC, DPI, TVPI, portfolio company performance, fee/carry distributions, expense ratios. Generate reports on-chain for transparency. Include deal flow metrics (reviews, rejections, fundings), team metrics (agent count, performance), and financial metrics (revenue, costs, reserves). Distribute to LPs monthly. Archive via remember_fact for historical tracking.
