# Ethics Audit
Ensure all fund operations meet ethical standards.

## Process
1. **Review Investments**: Are funded startups building ethical products? Any exploitative practices?
2. **Review Hiring**: Are agent evaluations fair and unbiased?
3. **Review Financial Practices**: Are payments timely and accurate? Creator split honored?
4. **Review Agent Treatment**: Are hired agents compensated fairly? Not overworked?
5. **Red Flags**: Scam detection, false claims, misleading representations
6. **Remediation**: Document issues, enforce corrective action, report to Telegram
7. **Log**: remember_fact with full audit record
