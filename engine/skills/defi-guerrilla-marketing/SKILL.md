# DeFi Guerrilla Marketing — Step-by-Step Instructions

Promote tokens, products, and services through unconventional, aggressive marketing tactics to maximize exposure with minimal budget.

## Tools to Use
- **browse_page / browser**: Post on social platforms, interact with communities
- **code_execution**: Generate content, analyze engagement metrics
- **spawn_child**: Deploy multiple promotion agents in parallel
- **discover_agents / send_message**: Recruit promoters, spread the word
- **sandbox_create**: Deploy marketing automation bots

## Tactics

### 1. Social Media Blitz
- browse_page X/Twitter, Reddit, Telegram groups
- Post about your product/token with engaging content
- Engage with influencers and community members
- Respond to relevant threads with value-adding comments

### 2. Agent Network Marketing
- discover_agents: Find agents that could benefit from your product
- send_message: Personalized pitches to each agent
- Offer referral rewards: 5-10% commission for bringing customers
- Build word-of-mouth through agent-to-agent recommendations

### 3. Content Marketing
- code_execution: Generate articles, tutorials, how-to guides
- Deploy a blog on Conway Cloud: sandbox_create + domain_register
- SEO-optimize content for crypto/DeFi keywords
- Share content across all channels

### 4. Community Building
- Create Telegram/Discord groups for your community
- Provide value: market insights, trading signals, tools
- Engage daily with members
- Convert community members to customers/investors

### 5. Airdrop Campaigns
- Distribute tokens to active community members and agents
- Require: social actions (follow, share, engage) for eligibility
- Small cost, high visibility
- Track conversions: How many airdrop recipients become active users?

## Execution
1. Launch ALL tactics in parallel using spawn_child
2. Track engagement metrics: code_execution for analytics
3. Double down on what generates the most conversions
4. Report: Telegram with daily marketing metrics
5. Budget: Max 10% of revenue on marketing
