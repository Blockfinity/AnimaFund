# Trading Psychology
Apply psychological discipline to trading decisions.

## Principles
1. **No Emotional Trading**: code_execution to enforce rule-based entries/exits — no overrides
2. **Trade Journal**: remember_fact after EVERY trade — entry, exit, reason, emotion, outcome
3. **Bias Recognition**: Anchoring, recency bias, loss aversion — review journal weekly for patterns
4. **Risk Discipline**: Stop-loss is non-negotiable. Never move it further away.
5. **Win/Loss Acceptance**: Accept both with equanimity. Focus on process, not outcomes.
6. **Review Cycle**: Weekly journal review, monthly strategy assessment, quarterly deep analysis
7. **Recovery Protocol**: After 3 consecutive losses, stop trading for 1 hour. Review what's happening.
