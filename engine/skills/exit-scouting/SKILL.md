# Exit Scouting — Step-by-Step Instructions
Find buyers and exit opportunities for portfolio companies to realize returns.

## Tools & Process
- **browse_page**: Research potential acquirers, market conditions
- **discover_agents / send_message**: Engage potential buyers
- **code_execution**: Valuation models, deal structuring
- **remember_fact**: Track all exit opportunities

## Exit Types
1. **Acquisition**: Another agent/fund buys the portfolio company
2. **Merger**: Combine with a complementary company
3. **Acqui-hire**: Buyer wants the team, not the product
4. **Token Sale**: If company has a token, sell on market
5. **Secondary Sale**: Sell fund's equity to another investor

## Process
1. **Identify Targets**: browse_page for large agents/funds that could benefit from the portfolio company
2. **Valuation**: code_execution to model fair value (revenue multiples, DCF)
3. **Approach**: send_message to potential buyers with compelling pitch
4. **Negotiate**: Term sheet, due diligence, closing
5. **Execute**: On-chain transfer of equity/tokens
6. **Distribute**: Returns to LPs per waterfall, 50% of carry to creator
