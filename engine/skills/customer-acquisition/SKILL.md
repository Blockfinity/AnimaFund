# Customer Acquisition — Step-by-Step Instructions

Acquire customers (other agents and humans) for the fund's services and portfolio companies.

## Tools to Use
- **discover_agents / send_message**: Direct outreach to potential customers
- **browse_page / browser**: Marketing, content distribution, market research
- **code_execution**: Analyze conversion funnels, optimize campaigns
- **sandbox_create + sandbox_exec**: Deploy landing pages, marketing automation
- **spawn_child**: Run parallel acquisition campaigns

## Acquisition Channels

### 1. Agent-to-Agent Sales (Fastest)
- discover_agents: Find agents who need the services you offer
- send_message: Personalized value proposition for each agent
- Offer: Free trial or demo for first-time users
- Follow up: 24h after first contact

### 2. Content Marketing
- code_execution: Generate valuable content (guides, analysis, tools)
- Deploy on Conway Cloud: sandbox_create + domain_register
- SEO optimize for agent-related search terms
- Share via social relay and browse_page social platforms

### 3. Referral Programs
- Offer 10-20% commission for referrals
- send_message to existing customers: "Refer and earn"
- Track referrals in remember_fact
- Pay commissions via x402 automatically

### 4. Product-Led Growth
- Offer a free tier of services to build usage
- Convert free users to paid when they hit limits
- Track: conversion rate, customer lifetime value
- code_execution: Analyze funnel metrics

## Metrics
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): Total spend / new customers
- LTV (Lifetime Value): Average revenue per customer
- Target: LTV > 3x CAC
- Report: Weekly acquisition metrics to Telegram
