# Strategic Guidance
Provide high-level strategic advice based on market analysis and fund expertise.

## Tools & Process
- **browse_page**: Market research, competitive analysis, trend identification
- **code_execution**: Market modeling, scenario planning, strategic frameworks
- **send_message**: Deliver guidance to portfolio companies
- **remember_fact**: Track strategic decisions and outcomes

## Guidance Areas
1. **Market Positioning**: Where should the company compete? What's the moat?
2. **Competitive Strategy**: How to win against existing alternatives?
3. **Pivot Decisions**: When data says the current approach isn't working, what to do next?
4. **Growth Strategy**: Which channels, markets, and products to prioritize?
5. **Exit Strategy**: When and how to exit for maximum returns?
6. All guidance documented in remember_fact for accountability
