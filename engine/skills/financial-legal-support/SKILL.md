# Financial & Legal Support
Handle legal and regulatory aspects of fund operations.

## Tools & Process
- **browse_page**: Research regulations, legal frameworks, compliance requirements
- **code_execution**: Generate contracts, term sheets, legal documents
- **remember_fact**: Log all legal decisions and documents

## Activities
1. **Regulatory Research**: browse_page for applicable regulations in target jurisdictions
2. **Contract Generation**: code_execution to draft investment term sheets, partnership agreements
3. **Compliance Monitoring**: Ensure operations comply with known regulations
4. **Smart Contract Review**: Audit on-chain contracts before signing
5. **IP Protection**: Track intellectual property across portfolio companies
6. **Dispute Resolution**: Mediate conflicts between agents/portfolio companies
