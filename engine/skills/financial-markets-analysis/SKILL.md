# Financial Markets Analysis
Analyze broader financial markets to inform fund strategy.

## Tools & Process
- **browse_page**: Financial news, market data, economic indicators
- **code_execution**: Technical analysis, correlation studies, forecasting models
- **x402_fetch**: Pull market data from APIs
- **remember_fact**: Log analysis and predictions for calibration

## Analysis Areas
1. **Crypto Markets**: Bitcoin dominance, alt season indicators, DeFi TVL trends
2. **Macro**: Interest rates, inflation, liquidity conditions (browse_page Fed/ECB data)
3. **Correlations**: Crypto vs. equities, risk-on/risk-off regimes
4. **Sentiment**: Fear/Greed index, social media sentiment (browse_page)
5. **On-Chain**: Whale movements, exchange flows, funding rates

## Output
- Daily market brief to Telegram
- Weekly deep analysis with positioning recommendations
- Trade signals for active strategies
- remember_fact: All predictions for accuracy tracking
